About the Author

Sophie Baek

Sophie Baek is a Berlin-born travel writer and photographer who quit her job in UX design in 2022 to do the thing everyone says they'll do and almost never does. Since then she has spent between two weeks and three months in each city she writes about — long enough to have a regular coffee order, short enough to still notice things locals stopped seeing years ago.

Her guides are not sponsored. The restaurants she recommends paid her nothing. The hotels she stayed in did not know she was coming. She has a particular weakness for markets, Soviet architecture, and places that are genuinely difficult to get to. She also has opinions about airport lounges that are frankly not appropriate here.
